TalkNix Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

TalkNix is a social networking platform developed by VirtuLink, a software division of ADDON, headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.

Welcome to TalkNix! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our social networking platform that enables connections, chatting, and engagement in online communities with messaging, activity feeds, content sharing, and digital marketplace features.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, username, password, profile picture
  • Content: Messages, posts, comments, shared media, and marketplace listings
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system
  • Payment Information: For marketplace transactions (processed by third-party providers)

2. Legal Compliance

TalkNix complies with:

  • India's Information Technology Act, 2000 and relevant rules
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents
  • Other applicable international data protection laws

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our social networking services
  • Enable messaging and community features
  • Facilitate digital marketplace transactions
  • Improve and personalize user experience
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security Measures

We implement:

  • End-to-end encryption for private messages
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure payment processing for marketplace transactions
  • Access controls and authentication protocols

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Request data portability
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • Lodge complaints with data protection authorities

6. International Data Transfers

As an India-based company serving global users, we implement Standard Contractual Clauses and other safeguards for international data transfers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of significant changes through platform notifications or email.
